My Hobbies

Bass

I've been playing music for quite a while now, but recently I've been learning how to play bass. I love rock music and would like to join a band at some point, but for now I'm just focusing on learning basic theory and practicing as much as I can. My favorite bass line right now is the one in The Internet's Special Affair off Ego Death.

Baking

I love cooking and baking when I have the time! My favorite cake to eat is Tres Leches, but when I made that I had to stand over a pot of carmel for like 2 hours and stir, so I'll probably take a few shortcuts next time. The most fun to make is probably an angel food cake -- its a merengue base, meaning the leavening agent is whipped egg whites. To get it out of the pan, you invert it onto a plate and just let it slide down (very satisfying).

Just before I left for college, I made French macarons, which was a lot of fun -- very labor intensive, but I think worth it. I packaged them into boxes and gave them out as thank-you gifts to people who helped me with college applications.

Video Games

Like most CS majors, I love playing video games! Recently, I've been playing a multiplayer shooter called Arc Raiders with some of my friends from high school. My favorite video game of all time is Portal, though RimWorld and Hades are not far behind. Surprisingly, I have little desire to use my degree to program my own game, but I think taking CS classes has helped me appreciate the technical details behind my favorite games with a lot more nuance.
